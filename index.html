FAQs

What is the Loyalty Program?
Our Loyalty Program is a rewarding system designed to appreciate our players for staying active. By placing bets, you earn points that help you climb through loyalty levels and unlock exclusive benefits. Higher levels grant access to enhanced bonuses, increased cashback, special promotions, and other premium rewards tailored to elevate your gaming experience.
How to increase my loyalty level?
The more you play at WEI$$.BET, the faster you can level up and unlock all the top-tier benefits. Stay with us and enjoy the ultimate gaming experience!
How many bets do I need to place to earn 1 point?
The number of bets required to earn 1 point depends on the game type and the current exchange rate. Points are calculated using the following formula:
Points Amount = (Bet Amount × Bet Wager) / Exchange Rate
Where:
◦ Bet Wager - a coefficient that depends on the type of game you are playing.
◦ Exchange Rate - the current actual rate of 1 WEFT.
You can view all Bet Wagers in the Play to Earn section on the Analytics page.
For example, to earn 1 point at Slots you need to bet ~15 USDT, in Live Games ~150 USDT.
The more points you collect, the higher your loyalty level becomes.
What is Level up bonus?
To celebrate our players' progress, we grant a special Level Up Bonus each time they reach a new level. Depending on the level reached, this bonus may include free spins, cash rewards. The higher the level, the better the bonus - each milestone unlocks valuable rewards for our players.
What is daily cashback?
Daily Cashback is a partial return of your lost funds for the previous day - a kind of ‘insurance’ that helps you stay in the game and try your luck again. The amount depends on your activity and loyalty level. You can receive from 5% to 13% of your losses back on a regular basis.
